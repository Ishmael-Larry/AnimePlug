My First Anime Page

This is a sample Anime blog post. I am excited to share with you ongoing Animes and new releases.

The story begins with Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is massacred by demons and his sister Nezuko is turned into a demon. Tanjiro trains under Sakonji Urokodaki and learns Water Breathing techniques. He passes the Final Selection and becomes an official Demon Slayer, traveling with Nezuko (who fights her demonic urges) to find a cure for her condition and hunt the demon who killed their family - Muzan Kibutsuji.
